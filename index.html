Connection Communities

Strait Area Transit is dedicated to bridging the gaps between communities in the Strait Area, providing a vital transportation link that fosters connection, accessibility, and economic growth. By offering reliable and affordable door-to-door transportation, we empower residents to access essential services, employment opportunities, and social activities, ultimately enhancing their quality of life. Our community-driven approach ensures that our services are tailored to meet the unique needs of our riders, promoting a sense of belonging and inclusivity throughout the region. By connecting communities, we are building a stronger, more resilient Strait Area, where everyone has the opportunity to thrive
